TITLE: AI-Powered-Parametric-Income-Protection-for-E-commerce-Delivery-Partners


PROBLEM STATEMENT : 
       Amazon and Flipkart, India's e-commerce delivery partners, form the core of the digital supply chain. However, outside disturbances like severe weather, pollution, floods, and unforeseen public curfews can completely stop their deliveries, costing them 20–30% of their monthly income. Since they don't have a set salary and must meet rigorous daily goals, every missed workday has an immediate effect on their ability to make a living. These workers currently have no income protection system in place to guard against such unforeseen circumstances; they are left to bear the entire financial loss without any kind of safety net.


PROPOSED SOLUTION : 
      ShieldKart is a parametric insurance web platform with AI capabilities designed for e-commerce delivery partners. It continuously scans the worker's active delivery zone for environmental and social disruption signals, such as temperature, rainfall, AQI levels, flood alerts, and civic curfews. Without the employee's involvement, ShieldKart automatically verifies the worker's active weekly policy, determines the precise amount of income lost based on their registered hourly rate, performs a fraud detection check, and processes an instant UPI payout when a disruption trigger exceeds a predetermined threshold. Weekly premiums are determined by a machine learning model that takes into account the worker's city, delivery zone risk score, past disruption frequency, and working hours. This ensures that coverage is reasonable, equitable, and in line with gig workers' earnings.


TOOLS USED : 
      React.js  , Python (FastAPI) , PostgreSQL , Redis , Celery , scikit-learn , OpenWeatherMap API  , CPCB AQI API , Government Alert API (Mock) , Razorpay (Test Mode) , Docker


EXPECTED OUTCOME : 
      A web platform where e-commerce delivery partners can register, choose a weekly insurance plan, and receive automatic UPI payouts when external disruptions like floods, extreme heat, or curfews are detected — with no manual claims. Includes an AI-driven premium engine, fraud detection, a worker dashboard showing coverage and payout history, and an admin dashboard with claim analytics and disruption forecasts.
